# Business Requirements

## Project Context

The environment supports a small business with operational requirements spanning:

- Office productivity
- Manufacturing workflows
- Shared file access
- Wireless connectivity
- Surveillance systems
- Remote access requirements

As the environment expanded organically over time, several infrastructure modernization requirements became necessary to support future operational growth and long-term maintainability.

---

## Core Business Objectives

### Reliability

Business operations require stable and predictable access to:

- Shared files
- Network resources
- Wireless connectivity
- Production systems
- Internet connectivity

Infrastructure downtime directly impacts operational efficiency and employee productivity.

---

### Scalability

The environment must support future growth in areas such as:

- Additional networked devices
- Expanded wireless coverage
- Increased storage capacity
- Additional workstations
- Future infrastructure services

The modernization effort prioritizes scalable architecture rather than short-term reactive upgrades.

---

### Security

The business requires improved security boundaries between operational environments.

Key goals include:

- Guest network isolation
- IoT segmentation
- Infrastructure protection
- Reduced lateral network exposure
- Simplified firewall policy management

---

### Manageability

Operational simplicity and centralized visibility are important long-term objectives.

Requirements include:

- Simplified infrastructure management
- Standardized hardware platforms
- Consistent network configuration
- Improved troubleshooting visibility
- Better operational documentation

---

### Storage & Data Integrity

Business operations rely heavily on shared storage access.

Requirements include:

- Improved storage reliability
- Redundancy planning
- Long-term scalability
- Backup strategy improvements
- Reduced single points of failure

---

### Physical Infrastructure Improvements

The physical network environment requires improved organization and standardization.

Desired improvements include:

- Structured rack infrastructure
- Improved cable management
- Patch panel standardization
- UPS integration
- Cleaner serviceability

---

## Technical Requirements

| Category | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Networking | VLAN-capable managed infrastructure |
| Wireless | Segmented SSID architecture |
| Security | Trust boundary enforcement |
| Storage | Redundant NAS architecture |
| Power | UPS-backed critical systems |
| Documentation | Centralized operational documentation |
| Scalability | Growth-ready infrastructure design |

---

## Long-Term Objectives

The modernization effort is intended to establish a foundation for future improvements such as:

- Centralized monitoring
- Improved remote management
- Virtualization platforms
- Advanced backup workflows
- Infrastructure standardization
- Improved disaster recovery planning

---

## Summary

The primary goal of this project is to transition the environment from an organically-grown operational network into a scalable, maintainable, and security-focused business infrastructure platform.

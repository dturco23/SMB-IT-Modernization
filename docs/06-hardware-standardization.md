# Hardware Standardization

## Purpose

This document outlines the proposed hardware standardization strategy for the SMB modernization project.

The objective is to reduce complexity, improve supportability, and create a consistent infrastructure platform.

---

## Standardization Goals

- Reduce mixed-vendor complexity
- Improve centralized management
- Standardize switching and wireless platforms
- Improve rack organization
- Support future growth
- Improve troubleshooting speed
- Establish repeatable deployment patterns

---

## Gateway / Firewall

### Proposed Role

The gateway/firewall provides:

- Internet edge routing
- Inter-VLAN routing
- Firewall policy enforcement
- VPN or remote access support
- Centralized traffic visibility

### Desired Capabilities

- VLAN support
- High throughput
- Centralized management
- Firewall rule visibility
- Remote management support
- Growth-ready hardware platform

---

## Switching

### Proposed Role

Switching infrastructure should support:

- VLAN-aware switching
- PoE for access points and devices
- Centralized port management
- Future port expansion
- Clean patch panel integration

### Desired Capabilities

- Managed switch platform
- PoE support
- 48-port capacity planning
- Trunk and access port configuration
- Centralized visibility

---

## Wireless

### Proposed Role

Wireless infrastructure should support segmented access for:

- Trusted business devices
- Guest users
- IoT systems
- Media devices
- Lab or testing devices

### Desired Capabilities

- VLAN-aware SSID mapping
- Centralized AP management
- Guest isolation
- Scalable coverage planning
- Consistent wireless policy enforcement

---

## Rack Infrastructure

Recommended improvements include:

- Enclosed or protected rack where appropriate
- Patch panel standardization
- Horizontal cable management
- Rack-mounted UPS
- Rack-mounted PDU
- Clear device labeling
- Serviceable cable routing

---

## Storage Hardware

Storage modernization should prioritize:

- Redundant disk layout
- ECC memory where possible
- 10Gb-ready architecture
- Reliable NAS platform
- Backup integration
- Future capacity expansion

---

## Summary

Hardware standardization improves reliability, simplifies administration, and creates a stronger foundation for future infrastructure growth.
